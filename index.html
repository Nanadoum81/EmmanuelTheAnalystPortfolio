I am Emmanuel Nanadoum, a Data Analyst/Business Analyst with expertise in SQL (SQL Server, MySQL, PostgreSQL), Python (Pandas, NumPy, SciPy, MatPlotLib), Tableau, Excel (VLOOKUP, Conditional Formatting, Pivot Tables), Microsoft Azure (DataBricks, Azure Data Lake, Azure Data Warehouse), Google Analytics, and Microsoft Power BI.

In my recent projects, I utilized my skills to analyze movie correlation, hotel revenue, Nashville housing data patterns, and COVID-19 vaccine tracker. I conducted ETL processes, developed data models, and created dashboards to help clients visualize the insights. Additionally, I provided technical insights to clients on how they can fully utilize their data and translated business questions and requirements into analysis plans.

Currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Data Management D& at WGU, I hold a certification in Google Analytics. My passion for data analytics, problem-solving, and data-driven decision-making fuels my motivation to learn and grow in this field.
